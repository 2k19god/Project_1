Processing Data in Terminal

A computer terminal is an electronic or electromechanical hardware device that is used for entering data into, and displaying or printing data from, a computer or a computing system. The’re many types of terminals. Terminals often consist of just a keyboard and monitor, with a connection to another computer. The purpose of a terminal is not to process information, but to send commands to another system. With modern computers, the word "terminal" usually refers to a terminal program, or emulator, which provides a text-based interface for typing commands.
